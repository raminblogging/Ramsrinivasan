I'm Ram Srinivasan — a Supply Chain professional based in Chennai, at the intersection of operations, technology, and continuous learning. I'm on a journey to blend domain expertise with the power of AI and automation.

My story

More than just a résumé

I started my career deep in supply chain operations — tracking inventory, optimizing flows, solving logistics puzzles that never quite fit neatly into spreadsheets. That friction is what drove me toward automation.

Over 5+ years, I've seen how smart systems can replace hours of manual work with minutes of intelligent processing. I became obsessed with building those systems — first for myself, then for the teams around me.

Today, I sit at an interesting crossroads: a practitioner who knows the operational pain points and has the technical curiosity to build solutions for them.

I believe the best tools are the ones that disappear into the work — so well-designed that they feel invisible. That philosophy drives how I approach both process design and the automation tools I build.

When I'm not untangling supply chain complexity, I'm usually drawing (analog, deliberate, slow) or deep in a game that rewards systems thinking. Both hobbies, oddly enough, teach the same lessons.

This site is where I share what I learn — from supply chain insights to automation experiments to the occasional half-formed idea worth thinking through in public.
